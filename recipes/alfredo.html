Alfredo

Description

A classic alfredo requires three elements: Chicken, Noodles, and Sauce. This recipe explains how to make a simple cheesey alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesean cheese
  • 1 tsp. salt

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in medium saucepan
  2. Add in garlic and cook for two minutes over medium heat
  3. Drop to a simmer and stir in cream
  4. Cook for 10 minutes or until sauce thickens
  5. Remove from heat
  6. Slowly add in cheese while whisking
  7. Whisk in salt to finish
